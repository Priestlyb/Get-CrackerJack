We share knowledge with the world

At Get Crackerjack, we’ve seen again and again how the seemingly simple act of creating can be a force for growth, change, and discovery in people’s lives. We want to inspire and multiply the kind of creative exploration that furthers expression, learning and application.


Get Crackerjack is an online learning community with thousands of classes for creative and curious people, on topics including illustration, design, photography, video, freelancing, and more. On Get Crackerjack, members come together to find inspiration and take the next step in their creative journey.
